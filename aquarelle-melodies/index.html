Aquarelle Melodies

Angelika

Mostly, I paint with oil paints and often add ripped paper or found objects such as feathers or petals for texture. When I travel, or sketch ideas, I like to use watercolour paint. 

These small paintings are  like melodies to a theme that popped into my head or I create them to play with a theme for an oil painting.
