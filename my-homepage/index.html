Born and raised in the vibrant city Berlin Germany, I have been exposed and involved in art making and learned from an early age to express myself visually. My dad once told me: "what's the use if you have a profession that makes you miserable because then you make everyone else around you miserable too!" Painting lets me get lost in imaginative stories that get transformed into a visual diary.. 

Art Education & Art Educator. There is always an opportunity to learn. My favourite years were, studying art at UMBC and again for as a graduate student at MICA (Maryland Institute College of Art). I have been living, creating art, and teaching to a broad community of learners with diverse backgrounds, cultures, and learning profiles. Living in the USA for almost 20 years, I visited Europe at least once a year collecting new ideas for my classroom and reconnecting with my personal roots.

Since 2020, I have been teaching teaching art and STEAM at the International School in Ulm/Neu-Ulm, Germany getting inspired by runs along the Donau and acquainted with the beautiful German life again, and spending more time with family and friends.
