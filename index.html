|| A Visual Diary ||  «angelika_paints«

On this website, you can find a selection of Angelika's colourful and eye catching art and see samples of her creative process.

Growing up in the vibrant German city Berlin, art making has been part of Angelika's life since early childhood watching her dad create linoleum cut prints and her mom doing drawings of nature. She has studied and taught art for many years in different locations worldwide. Oil and/or mixed media is her favourite media for painting. She enjoys the slow drying process of oil paints that gives her the freedom to create layers and depth that suites her expressive ideas. When creating sculptures, Angelika uses traditional low-fire clay for hand-built sculptures.

Art works are available by contacting Angelika directly or through Saatchi Online Gallery London. If you have something special in mind, Angelika welcomes commissions to create your personalised artwork.
